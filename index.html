Trezor.io/Start® - Starting Up Your Device® - Trezor®

Start your Trezor hardware wallet setup by visiting trezor.io/start. Connect your Trezor device to your computer using the provided USB cable. Download and install Trezor Bridge to enable secure communication. Follow the on-screen instructions to create a new wallet, set a PIN, and write down your recovery seed. Use the Trezor Suite app to manage your cryptocurrencies, ensuring your device firmware and software are up to date for maximum security. For comprehensive setup guidance and support, visit the official Trezor setup page​ (Trezor Security)​​
